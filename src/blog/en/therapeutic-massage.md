Therapeutic massage is manual treatment focused on improving body function, reducing tension, and supporting recovery. Unlike a purely relaxing massage, it begins with assessment and adapts to what the person needs that day.

## What it focuses on

It can address muscle pain, stiffness, limited mobility, postural overload, accumulated physical stress, or discomfort related to repetitive movement. The goal is not only temporary relief, but understanding what may be contributing to the problem.

## What to expect

A session usually includes an initial conversation, observation of the area being treated, and techniques adjusted to your tolerance. Pressure does not need to be extreme to be effective; the right stimulus matters more.

## After the session

It is common to feel lighter, move better, or experience deep rest. Some temporary sensitivity can happen, especially when there was a lot of accumulated tension.
