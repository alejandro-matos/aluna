A therapeutic massage usually lasts between 60 and 90 minutes, although the ideal length depends on the goal. Treating one specific area is different from doing a more complete session for several parts of the body.

## 60-minute sessions

These are a good option for a specific need, such as neck and shoulders, low back, or legs. They also work well for follow-up when there is already a treatment plan.

## 90-minute sessions

These allow more time to work calmly, integrate several areas, and understand how one discomfort connects with the rest of the body. They are often useful when tension is accumulated or several areas are involved.

## Quality before the clock

Duration helps, but it is not everything. An effective session combines assessment, appropriate technique, communication, and intensity that the body can process.
