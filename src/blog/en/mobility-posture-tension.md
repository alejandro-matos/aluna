Many body aches do not appear overnight. They often build through small repeated loads: hours sitting, emotional tension, poor rest, repetitive movements, or lack of pauses.

## Posture changes throughout the day

There is no single perfect posture. The issue is often staying in the same position for too long. The body needs variety, mobility, and recovery.

## Tension and the nervous system

When there is stress, the body can stay on alert. That increases muscle tension and makes areas like the neck, jaw, back, or shoulders feel more loaded.

## How manual therapy helps

A well-focused treatment can reduce stiffness, improve movement, and help you recognize patterns that may need to change outside the session.
